Protect Nature

with a Donation

Do your part to create a more sustainable future for our planet. The power to protect and restore nature—now and for the next generation—is in your hands.

Here are some reputable organizations and groups where you can donate money to support environmental causes.

The Nature Conservancy

Works to conserve the lands and waters on which all life depends. They address the most pressing conservation threats at the largest scale.

Link: The Nature Conservancy

Wildlife Fund (WWF)

Focuses on conserving nature and reducing the most pressing threats to the diversity of life on Earth.

Link: World Wildlife Fund

Greenpeace

An independent global campaigning organization that acts to change attitudes and behaviors, to protect and conserve the environment, and to promote peace.

Link:Greenpeace

Environmental Defense Fund (EDF)

Works to solve the most critical environmental problems facing the planet, focusing on areas where they can make the biggest impact.

Link: Environmental Defense Fund

Sierra Club Foundation

Promotes climate solutions, conservation, and movement building through a powerful combination of strategic philanthropy and grassroots advocacy.

Link: Sierra Club Foundation

Rainforest Alliance

Works to conserve biodiversity and ensure sustainable livelihoods by transforming land-use practices, business practices, and consumer behavior.

Link: Rainforest Alliance

Friends of the Earth

A network of environmental organizations in 74 countries, working to promote sustainable development and environmental conservation.

Link: Friends of the Earth

Ocean Conservancy

Works to protect the ocean from today’s greatest global challenges, creating science-based solutions for a healthy ocean and the wildlife and communities that depend on it.

Link: Ocean Conservancy

350.org

Focuses on reducing atmospheric CO2 to safe levels through a global grassroots movement to solve the climate crisis.

Link: 350.org

National Resources Defense Council (NRDC)

Works to safeguard the earth—its people, its plants and animals, and the natural systems on which all life depends.

Link: NRDC
